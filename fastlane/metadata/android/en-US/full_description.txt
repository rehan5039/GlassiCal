GlassiCal is a modern, open-source calculator application featuring a stunning glassmorphism UI.

Features:
*   Glassmorphism UI: A sleek, transparent, and blurry aesthetic.
*   Essential Functions: Basic arithmetic (+, -, *, /) and percentage calculations.
*   Adaptive Design: Fits perfectly on devices with gesture or 3-button navigation.
*   Privacy Focused: No internet permission required.

Based on OpenCalc. Licensed under GPLv3.
